About Us

Save the Kids is a non governmental organization working towards making the world a better place for the underprivileged children. We are Delhi’s leading independent child rights’ NGO, which works in the Delhi and NCR region. Beginning its journey in April 2018, we have changed the lives of thousands of children.We firmly believe that every child deserves the best chance for a bright future.
